Daisy Potholder Pattern
MATERIALS
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET AND KNITTING COTTON 89 yds. White.
"STAR" PEARL COTTON, SIZE 5 49 yds. Green, and 20 yds. Yellow.
Steel Crochet Hook No. 7.
1st Row: With Yellow, ch 6, join to form a ring, ch 4 and work 23 trc into ring, join in 4th st of ch, break thread.
2nd Row: Attach White under ch on wrong side of work and work 1 sc over each st taking up entire st, join.
Next Row:
Start Petals: Ch 4, 3 trc in same st, 2 trc in each of the next 2 sts, 4 trc in next st, ch 4, turn.
Next Row: 4 trc in trc, 1 trc in each of the remaining trc, 4 trc in 4th st of ch, ch 4, turn.
Next Row: Start decreasing, * thread over twice, insert in next st, pull through and work off 2 loops twice, repeat from * twice, thread over and work off remaining loops at one time, 1 trc in each of the next 11 sts, * thread over twice, insert in next st, pull through and work off 2 loops twice, repeat from * 3 times, thread over and work off remaining loops at one time, ch 4, turn.
Next Row: * Thread over twice, insert in next st, pull through and work off 2 loops twice, repeat from * twice, thread over and work off remaining loops at one time, 1 trc in each of the next 7 sts, decrease next 4 sts in same manner, ch 4, turn. Next Row: Decrease in same manner as beginning of last row, 1 trc in each of the next 3 sts, decrease next 4 sts, ch 4, turn.
Next Row: * Thread over twice, insert in next st, pull through and work off 2 loops twice, repeat from * 5 times, thread over and work off remaining loops at one time, ch 1, break thread.
Attach White in next sc and continue in same manner until there are 6 petals. With Yellow, starting in base of petal, work a row of sc all around petals, join, break thread. Tack petals together at 2nd row overlapping petals slightly.
2nd Row of Petals: Attach White in a Yellow sc below tacking of petals, ch 4 and work 7 trc in same space, ch 4, turn.
Next Row 3 trc in 1st trc, 2 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 4 trc, 2 trc in next trc, 4 trc in 4th st of ch, ch 4, turn.
Next 4 Rows Decrease in same manner as 1st petal, break thread.
With Yellow, work a row of sc all around petal, break thread and continue in same manner until there are 6 petals, and tack them together as on image.
Back: With White, ch 6, join to form a ring, ch 4, 4 trc in ring, * ch 1, 5 trc in ring, repeat from * twice, ch 1, join in 4th st of ch.
2nd Row: Ch 4, work 1 trc in each trc working 3 trc, ch 2, 3 trc in each corner, join in 4th st of ch.
Repeat 2nd row 3 times, break thread.
Attach Green and repeat 2nd row 3 times.
Next Row: * Ch 4, 2 trc in same space, skip 3 sts, sc in next st, repeat from * all around, working a loop in one corner, (loop: ch 20, sc in same space, work 30 sc over loop, sl st in same space) break thread.
Sew flower in position on back section as illustrated.
Next Row: Start decreasing, * thread over twice, insert in next st, pull through and work off 2 loops twice, repeat from * twice, thread over and work off remaining loops at one time, 1 trc in each of the next 11 sts, * thread over twice, insert in next st, pull through and work off 2 loops twice, repeat from * 3 times, thread over and work off remaining loops at one time, ch 4, turn.
Next Row: * Thread over twice, insert in next st, pull through and work off 2 loops twice, repeat from * twice, thread over and work off remaining loops at one time, 1 trc in each of the next 7 sts, decrease next 4 sts in same manner, ch 4, turn.
Next Row: Decrease in same manner as beginning of last row, 1 trc in each of the next 3 sts, decrease next 4 sts, ch 4, turn.
Next Row: * Thread over twice, insert in next st, pull through and work off 2 loops twice, repeat from * 5 times, thread over and work off remaining loops at one time, ch 1, break thread.
Attach White in next sc and continue in same manner until there are 6 petals. With Yellow, starting in base of petal, work a row of sc all around petals, join, break thread. Tack petals together at 2nd row overlapping petals slightly.
2nd Row: of Petals-Attach White in a Yellow sc below tacking of petals, ch 4 and work 7 trc in same space, ch 4, turn.
Next Row: 3 trc in 1st trc, 2 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 4 trc, 2 trc in next trc, 4 trc in 4th st of ch, ch 4, turn. Next 4 Rows: Decrease in same manner as 1st petal, break thread.
With Yellow, work a row of sc all around petal, break thread and continue in same manner until there are 6 petals, and tack them together as illustrated.
Back. With White: ch 6, join to form a ring, ch 4, 4 trc in ring, * ch 1, 5 trc in ring, repeat from * twice, ch 1, join in 4th st of ch. 2nd Row: Ch 4, work 1 trc in each trc working 3 trc, ch 2, 3 trc in each corner, join in 4th st of ch.
Repeat 2nd row 3 times, break thread.
Attach Green and repeat 2nd row 3 times.
Next Row: * Ch 4, 2 trc in same space, skip 3 sts, sc in next st, repeat from * all around, working a loop in one corner, (loop: ch 20, sc in same space, work 30 sc over loop, sl st in same space) break thread. Sew flower in position on back section as in image.
